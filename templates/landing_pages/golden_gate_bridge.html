Golden Gate Bridge Tour

About your host

I'm a professional Captain with an expansive career that has taken me from Australia to Canada and now USA. I have run SCUBA dive tours, Jet Boat thrill rides, fishing, whale watching and simple fun on the ocean trips for over 10 years. I am first aid trained with all tours having an emphasis on safety first. .

What we'll do

Embarking at Bluewater Yacht Harbor in Sausalito, you will receive a full safety brief and the boat tour. Departing the marina, we will cruise along the Sausalito foreshore and out under the iconic Golden Gate Bridge for that 'Postcard photo.'

What else you should know

Seasickness is a real thing that can hit anyone in any condition, if you are at all unsure please take precautions in advance and guarantee to not be sabotaged by the green feeling, it is avoidable.
